# Plan and host Alex's 40th — end-to-end

## Technical Approach

Run a 6-week countdown. Lock the venue + caterer first (Week 1
decide-first task), then outline the guest list and invitations
(Week 2), then iterate on the run-sheet through Weeks 3–5 as RSVPs
come in. Weeks 5–6 handle the day-of logistics, the actual party,
and the immediate post-party follow-up (photos, thank-yous).

AI assists with: invitation copy, guest-list research from social
media, caterer comparison spreadsheet, run-sheet template, speech
draft. The human does: the booking, the guest contact, the secret-
keeping, and the actual hosting on the night.

## Risks & Assumptions

- **Assumption**: 30 invites yields 25+ on the night (typical 80%
  RSVP rate for close-circle parties).
- **Assumption**: Alex's sister has time across all 6 weeks for
  ~2 hours/week of help.
- **Risk**: Venue cancels last-minute. Backup: pre-identify two
  alternates and keep contact details on file.
- **Risk**: Information leak via mutual friend. Mitigation: small
  trusted helper group (3 people max) and no group chat that
  includes anyone who might see Alex's phone.
